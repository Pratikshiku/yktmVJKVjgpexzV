## Beauty Shopping Website

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/2b78176dcafa48fe8133289721ef232e/ghb20250917) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript` etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (Any one of the listed software can run)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Function Description
The system is divided into (roles): Regular User, Merchant, Administrator.

**Regular User Functions:**
1. Login
2. Register
3. Homepage
4. Forum
5. Products
6. Announcement Information
7. Personal Center
8. Delivery Address
9. Product Favorites
10. Product Orders




**Merchant Functions:**
1. Login
2. Register
3. Change Password
4. Personal Information
5. Forum Management
6. Product Management
7. Product Review Management
8. Product Favorites Management
9. Product Order Management
10. Announcement Information Management




**Administrator Functions:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center (Change Password, Personal Information)
3. Administrator Management
4. Product Type Management
5. Announcement Type Management
6. Member Level Management
7. Merchant Credit Type Management
8. Forum Management
9. Product Management
10. Product Review Management
11. Product Favorites Management
12. Product Order Management
13. Announcement Information Management
14. Merchant Management
15. User Management
16. Carousel Management
